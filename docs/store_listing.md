# NeoPill Store Listing Draft

## App Name
NeoPill

## Short Description
Simple medication reminders, dose tracking, refill alerts, and doctor-ready reports.

## Full Description
NeoPill helps people stay consistent with treatment in a calm, easy-to-read interface.

What NeoPill offers:
- Clear daily schedule focused on what to take now
- Medication reminders and dose history
- Refill awareness for low stock medications
- Comfort mode with larger text and calmer visuals
- Doctor-ready PDF reports
- Support for caregivers and family helpers

NeoPill is designed to reduce confusion, lower missed doses, and make everyday treatment easier to follow.
