# Pillora Store Listing Draft

## App Name
Pillora

## Short Description
Medication and supplement reminders, daily routine tracking, and doctor-ready reports.

## Full Description
Pillora helps people stay consistent with treatment in a calm, easy-to-read interface.

What Pillora offers:
- Clear daily timeline focused on what to take now and what comes next
- Medication and supplement reminders in one routine-friendly flow
- Smart reminders with calmer wording for treatment and wellness courses
- Dose history, adherence tracking, and refill awareness
- Comfort mode with larger text and calmer visuals
- Doctor-ready PDF reports
- Support for complex schedules with different doses across the day

Pillora is designed to reduce confusion, lower missed doses, and make everyday treatment easier to follow.
