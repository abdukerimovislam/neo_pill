# Pillora Privacy Policy Draft

This draft should be reviewed and published at a public URL before release.

## What Pillora stores
- Medication and supplement course details
- Reminder schedules and notification settings
- Dose history and adherence logs
- Optional medicine photos you choose to add

## How data is used
- To show your daily schedule and reminder timeline
- To generate refill warnings and progress summaries
- To create doctor-ready PDF reports when you request them

## Data location
- Core health data is stored locally on the device
- Shared reports are exported only when the user chooses to share them

## Permissions
- Notifications: used for medication and supplement reminders
- Camera and photos: used only if the user adds medicine images
